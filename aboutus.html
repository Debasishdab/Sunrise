WELCOME TO SUNRISE HOSPITAL

Founded on a vision of delivering compassionate healthcare, Sunrise Hospital embarked on its journey. From its inception, the hospital aimed to redefine medical excellence, becoming a trusted name in the healthcare .

Over the years, Sunrise Hospital has evolved, adapting to dynamic healthcare challenges, and emerging as a beacon of hope for patients seeking advanced and personalized medical solutions.

As we reflect on our history, we take pride in the dedicated professionals, including esteemed medical directors Dr. Muruli DC and Dr. Roopa, who have been instrumental in shaping Sunrise Hospital into what it is today.

From pioneering surgical techniques to embracing cutting-edge technologies, our history is a testament to our unwavering commitment to advancing healthcare, ensuring that each patient's journey is guided by expertise, empathy, and a pursuit of optimal well-being.

Mission & Vision
